Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2025

Overview

Welcome to Misolfa, our Sheet Music Workout app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to be clear about our data practices: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

Our app is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track user behavior
  • We do not require user registration
  • All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Play Families Policy and do not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Updates to Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
